Tutorials

Follow the steps below to build your project:

Step 1: Connect Arduino to your breadboard.

Step 2: Insert the microphone module into the breadboard.

Step 3: Connect jumper wires from the module to Arduino pins.

Step 4: Add LEDs and resistors to the breadboard.

Step 5: Upload the sample code in Arduino IDE.

🎉 Done! Your LEDs now blink with sound.
